ABOUT SUBTERAN

The 4th April 2015 saw the first instalment of Subteran in Manchester. Over the course of 2015 Subteran will be hosting a number of nights in Manchester for you to indulge yourself on your love of techno-fused house and underground techno. The night is derived from the passion of its resident Dj's to put a little back into a scene that Manchester lives and breathes. Our nights are designed around the music and you. Nothing more!!

To kick things off we held a residents party from the RoadHouse in Manchester's Northern Quarter. Our headliner on the night was no other than Subteran resident and Manchester's very own MATT EVERSON. Matt is no stranger to the Manchester techno scene having graced the decks at the WHP every year since it began in 2007. You'll have seen him at Parklife over the last 3 years and his productions have made it on Radio 1's playlist and signed to Toolroom and Monique Musique. Our remaining residents kept the night flowing with their passion for the very best this music has to offer.

Having now sourced a permanent home for Subteran, we're VERY pleased to announce that Subteran will be hosted at no other than the basement at Joshua Brooks( Nominated for Best Small Club 2014 as part of DJ Mag’s Best of British Awards). The venue is amazing, looks amazing and sounds second to none! We're sure you'll love the venue as much as the music!

Tickets will be £5 all night with a concession for students during term time.

Subteran is about you and the music, nothing more!!

Our doors open at 11pm until 4am. For more info and a taste of what's in store visit our SubteranMcr pages on Twitter/Facebook/Instagram/Resident Advisor and .
